About Our Research Center

The Research Center of Cancer Translational Medicine (RCCTM) is a cancer research center established by Taipei Medical University.

The RCCTM aims to accelerate the development of new diagnostic tools and therapeutic approaches for cancer, bridging the gap between laboratory research and clinical applications.

Our center focuses on understanding cancer progression mechanisms, metastasis, and the identification of new therapeutic targets and biomarkers. We collaborate with leading international institutions to advance cancer research and improve patient outcomes.

The mission of RCCTM is to enhance cancer research through translational medicine approaches, foster international collaboration, and develop innovative solutions for cancer prevention, detection, and treatment.

2025 TMU-CWRU Symposium on Cancer Translational Research

The TMU Research Center of Cancer Translational Medicine (RCCTM) and Case Western Reserve University (CWRU) are pleased to announce the 2025 TMU-CWRU Symposium on Cancer Translational Research. This collaborative event brings together leading researchers, clinicians, and industry experts to share cutting-edge advances in cancer translational medicine.

March 27-28, 2025 | Taipei Medical University

Symposium Overview

The symposium will focus on advances in cancer translational research with special emphasis on:

  • Precision medicine approaches in cancer therapy
  • Novel biomarkers for early cancer detection
  • Innovative immunotherapy strategies
  • Artificial intelligence and machine learning in cancer research
  • Cancer metastasis mechanisms and interventions
  • Clinical trial design and implementation
